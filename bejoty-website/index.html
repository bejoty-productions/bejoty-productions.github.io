About

Bejoty Productions is a solo, indie game developer.

Games have always been my escape, a constant grounding force, and a way I connect with some of my best friends. From Hearthstone, to League of Legends, Stardew Valley, and countless others, a variety of games have helped to shape who I am.

I became a developer to continue passing that forward. My goal is to design experiences that feel fresh, with mechanics that invite deep strategy and replay-ability. I hope that my games can become a meaningful part of other peoples' lives.
